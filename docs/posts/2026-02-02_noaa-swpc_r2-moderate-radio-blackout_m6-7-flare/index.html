NOAA SWPC: M6.7 solar flare; R2 (Moderate) radio blackout alert

Published (UTC): 2026-02-02 19:01

Summary

  • NOAA’s Space Weather Prediction Center (SWPC) alerts include an X-ray event summary reporting an M6.7 solar flare on 2026-02-02, with NOAA scale R2 — Moderate (radio blackout impacts possible on the sunlit side of Earth).
  • SWPC also posted a watch that G1 (Minor) geomagnetic storming is possible on Feb 05 (per the watch text).

What happened (facts)

  • R2 (Moderate) radio blackout alert: SWPC “ALERT: X-Ray Flux exceeded M5” indicates threshold reached at 2026 Feb 02 1121 UTC (NOAA scale R2).
  • M6.7 flare summary: SWPC “SUMMARY: X-ray Event exceeded M5” reports begin/max/end times and class M6.7 (message includes begin 1115 UTC, max 1124 UTC, end 1131 UTC).
  • Geomagnetic watch: SWPC watch text says G1 (Minor) geomagnetic storm conditions are possible on Feb 05 (per message).
